In my youngest age, I started to play cello, bit by bit I developed an interest in the art of violon making. I got the opportunity to work in a baroque workshop and after a while I decided to make deeper my knowledge in the Newark School of Violin making. After my graduation, I got the big change to do some workexperience in Nelly Poidevin workshop where I discovered the fascinated world of baroque bow making straightly linked to the research and reconstruction on antique bows from different period. Since this time, I still explore the bow making world, inspired by the iconography and research in different museums.
